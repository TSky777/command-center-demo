# Command Center — Sales Pitch Script

**Live demo:** https://violatethedresscode.github.io/command-center-demo/
*(Click "Enter Demo" — loads instantly, works on phone or laptop, nothing to install.)*

---

## The 15-second hook

> "Most small businesses are flying blind. They've got Shopify in one tab, their
> ad accounts in three more, a spreadsheet for expenses, and no single number
> that tells them if they actually made money today. I build them one screen
> that pulls it all together — revenue, ad spend, real profit after costs,
> updated automatically. Want me to show you?"

Then open the live link.

---

## The demo walkthrough (2 minutes)

**Open the link and hit "Enter Demo."** Talk while it loads.

### 1. The Home tab — "This is their whole business on one screen."
- Point at **Order Revenue** and **Net Profit** side by side: *"Revenue is the
  vanity number. This one — net profit — is what's actually left after product
  cost, shipping, fees, and ad spend. Most owners can't tell you this number for
  today. Here it's live."*
- Tap the **date buttons** (Today / 7D / 30D): *"Any window, instantly."*
- Scroll to **Meta Ads** and **Google Ads**: *"Their ad spend and return on ad
  spend, pulled in automatically — no logging into five dashboards."*
- Point at **Attribution** and **LTV**: *"Where the money's actually coming
  from, and what a customer is worth over time."*

### 2. The Expenses tab — "And it knows their real costs."
- Click **+ Add Expense**, add something live (e.g. "Warehouse — $1,800/mo").
- *"Every recurring cost gets folded into that profit number automatically. No
  more guessing at the end of the month."*

**Land the point:** *"That's the core. One login, the real numbers, no
spreadsheet. Now — this is where it gets good for a business like yours."*

---

## The expansion pitch — "This is a starting point, not the whole thing."

> "What you just saw is the foundation. The platform is **modular** — I turn on
> what your business actually needs. Depending on what you're running, we can add:"

- **Influencer & affiliate tracking** — *"See exactly which creators and affiliates
  drive real sales and revenue, not just clicks. Know who to pay and who to cut."*
- **Product analytics** — *"Best and worst sellers, margin by product, what's
  trending up or down — so you stock what makes money."*
- **Team / staff performance** — *"If you've got sales reps, athletes, or
  ambassadors, track each person's numbers in their own view."*
- **Inventory forecasting & reorder alerts** — *"It watches your stock and sell-
  through speed and warns you before a bestseller goes out — with a suggested
  reorder date. No more selling out of your hero product at the worst time."*
- **Ad performance & attribution** — *"Deeper breakdowns across Meta, Google, and
  every channel, so every dollar is accounted for."*
- **Customer LTV & cohorts** — *"Who's coming back, who's churning, what a
  customer is really worth."*

> "We start with the core dashboard, and bolt these on as you grow. You're not
> paying for a bloated tool you don't use — you're getting exactly your business
> on one screen."

---

## Handling the common objections

- **"I already use [Shopify analytics / a spreadsheet]."**
  *"Those show you revenue. They don't show you profit after ad spend and costs,
  and they don't pull your ad accounts in. That's the gap this fills — and it's
  one screen instead of six tabs."*

- **"How much?"**
  *"Depends how many pieces you turn on. The core dashboard is [your price]. Each
  module is [your price]. I'll scope it to exactly what you need — no waste."*
  *(Set your own numbers. A common model: a setup fee + a monthly per-business
  hosting/maintenance fee, with add-on modules priced individually.)*

- **"Is my data safe / who else sees it?"**
  *"Every business gets its own private instance. Your numbers are yours — nobody
  else's data touches yours, ever."*

- **"I'm not technical."**
  *"You don't touch anything. I set it up, connect your store and ad accounts,
  and hand you a link and a login. That's it."*

---

## The close

> "Here's what I'd suggest: let me connect your real store to a private version
> of this so you're looking at *your* numbers, not the demo. Takes me a day. If
> the first time you see your real profit on one screen isn't worth it, we walk
> away. Sound fair?"

---

### Notes for you (the seller)
- Always demo on the **live link**, not screenshots. Letting them tap the date
  buttons themselves is what sells it.
- The demo numbers are a fictional "Demo Co." If you're pitching a specific
  industry, the seeded numbers can be re-skinned to look like their world
  (edit `server/data/kpis.js`, regenerate, redeploy).
- The modules above are real and buildable — they're not vapor. Lead with the
  one or two that fit the prospect, not the whole list.
